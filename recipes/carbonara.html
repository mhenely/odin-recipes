Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is one of the classic Roman pasta dishes that is equally simple as it is rich. Made with guanciale, eggs, cheese, cracked black pepper, and pasta, its simplicity makes it a favorite for cooks and patrons alike.

Ingredients

  • 280g spaghetti
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 200g guanciale in 1cm cubes
  • 30g grana padano, grated
  • 20g pecorino romano, grated

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in boiling water like mentioned on package
  2. Fry little guanciale cubes in order to make them crispy outside and soft inside
  3. Whisk egg yolks, grana padano, pecorino, and rendered guanciale fat obtained from frying in a bowl
  4. Let this mixture rest
  5. Once pasta is cooked, mix into the sauce, add black pepper, guanciale cubes, and starchy pasta water as needed to make it creamy
  6. Plate the pasta, sprinkling pecorino on top alongside additioanl black pepper
  7. Serve at a room temperature
